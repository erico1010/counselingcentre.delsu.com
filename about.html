About The Centre

The DELSU Counseling Centre under the watch of the director, provides a safe enviroment to help members of the DELSU community, particularly students explore the challenges of life through diversified programmes within the confines of counseling practice. The centre is typically a service centre but also serves as a training site for trainee mental health care givers and counselors undergoing practicum. The centre is actively engaged in community services but reserves exclusively, personal counseling services to currently enrolled students of the Delta State University.

As one of the many service arms of the University, the centre is fully accredited by CASSON and has a staff that is committed to excellence within the framework of its mandate. Though autonomous, the Centre is affiliated with The Department of Guidance and Counselling and draws a cross section of its consultants from there.

Opening hours are within week days. We are not open for consultation during week ends and throughout holidays but attends to every student who sees the need to connect with us through our online service platforms especially in crisis situations. Find us at The University Counseling Centre, beside Faculty of Pharmacy Complex, Ebonyi road, Site 3, Abraka Campus, Delta State University.
