Booking An Appointment With Delta State University Student Counseling Centre

We sincerely appologise for whatever inconveniences you went through to reach us. The Centre and its personnel identifies unreservedly with you and are committed to your well being within the confines of the counseling practice. We are actively engaged in a wide varienty of community services but this section is exclusively reserved to currently enrolled students of the university and staff. Please provide us with your data and we will be with you just in time.

We use the data provided to determine the nature of help you need and hence the appropriate personnel to meet with you. Your Delsu unique email means alot to us. The message section of this request has a lot to say about you, the challenge, and the anticipated help. We can understand whatever language you wrote. Write in the language best known to you!







Your request for an appointment with us means a lot to us so we will get back to you in no time. While we process your appointment you can explore other available options of getting help.
