About Our Online GPA Calculator

Our Online GPA Calculator is meant to help you solve the problems associated with manual calculation by improving your overall speed and accuracy. Developed by Obine Eric Chukwuka, a Guidance and Counseling student, the calculator works your GPA to a 5.00 Scale and requires certain inputs for full functionality.

DISCLAIMER: Neither the Centre nor the developer will accept responsibility for the usage of this calculator. Users descretion is adviced and you are admonished to make correct inputs
