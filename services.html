Services

What to expect from us

You can expect to meet with someone who will be geniunely interested in your concerns and will take you seriously. Depending on the time of booking your appointment and other prevailing circumstances, the centre will keep in wait, a therapist that will attend to you in whatever area of need within mental health practice and discuss openly with you. Your therapist is a professional counselor and will stick to the code and ethics of the counselling practice to see your challengesfully resolved with your cooperation. We care about your safety and respect your dignity as human so expect whatever information you revealed to your counselor to remain a confidential property. Your therapist in the process of helping you may use a blend of techniques and approaches depending on his/her training and area of expertise in all of which your rights will never be violated. Our counselling offices are designed and built to meet the purpose of counselling bearing your comfort, confidentiality, and safety in mind.

On issues of third parties, you are incharge of who can be invited into the therapeutic relationship if the need arises. For record sake and improving the therapeutic process, your counselor may have to audio tape, video tape, or use other means but in any case, your consent and approval will always be sought and the records will remain a property of the centre which it treats with confidentiality and refers to in future terms to enhance its services to you alone and you have full rights to challenge how the property is used should you feel other wise. Nonetheless, if the property is demanded by the Law for a legal action for or against, the centre, being bound by the State's laws will fall to the Law. Furthermore, if the Centre perceives, there are suicidal tendencies, threats to take another life, or public health threats, the Centre will in these situations liase with appropriate authorities to get help for you.

Services

What we expect from you

Counselling is a two-way-relationship. We try as much as poissible to help you within the standards of the counselling practice. We expect you cooperate with your therapist and discuss openly with them. Contrary to many conceptions, counselling is not magical mind reading. Your counselor being a professional works with data they are able to gather about you and the situation to help you explore and find solutions which implementation of, rests on your willingness and commitment. Time is precious and we must use it judiciously if we must be able to attend to the thousands of members of the DELSU community that we are mandated to serve. We therefore expect you to be punctual and regular at sessions. Ensure to communicate with the Centre if you wish to terminate an appointment within 24 hours of request to enable us allocate the space to another. We expect you communicate with your therapist any sign of improvements or compliactions if there be any as therapy progresses and also do same when no changes are noticed. Depending on your counselor's professional inclinations, tasks, assignments, recommendations, or referrals may be made in the lights of finding lasting solutions. In these, we expect your full cooperation and participation in the process.

Self termination of therapy is allowed. You have all rights to cease from receiving therapy if you so wish but we expect you notify us and follow due process. You have the option of choosing counselors who should attend to you. We expect you communicate with the Centre any difficulties or challenges you may have relating with your counselor and we will assign another to you.

Personal-Social Guidance

Our personal-social guidance services enables you to have self understanding with regards to relating with yourself and others in order to be able to deal effectively with and manage the psychological situations involved in interpersonal relationships. Vocational Guidance

Our vocational guidance services cover a wide range of programmes designed to help you assess your capabilities, interests, skills, aptitudes, and other qualities in order for you to know yourselves and make appropriate career choices. Some struggle to fit in, some under-perform, some quit, others never find gainful employment because the jobs they seek require a differnt kind persons that they are not despite the technical know hows they've acquired in the course of training or in many cases, their choice of profession are irrelevant in the society or the jobs they seek are non-existent in the economy. These have formed the basis for wide spread unemployment and under-employment across the globe. Hence, the need for vocational guidance.

We Can Help You With These Situations Through Therapy And Our Self-Help Resources

Relationship Problems

Relationships are beautiful and can come with its own unique challenges. We can help you through our relationship counseling services.

Academic Problems

Do you have any problems with your academics? Are you struggling with low academic grades? Are you having issues with poor comprehension? Do you you have exam phobia? We can help you through our academic counseling services.

Adjustment Problems

Cause: Poor transition between life stages, educational levels, residential enviroments, signifant events, relationships and many more. Untreated, they degenerate to further complications. If you have persistent symptoms of anxeity and depression after any transition make a date with us.

Family Problems

You are not alone in this. We understand just exactly how disturbing things can be when things are not right with one's home that's why we make sure our experienced counselors will work with you through challenges and solution exploration. We care about you.

Depression Treatment

Are you having persistent low mood and feel irritable in most things? Are you begining to or already lost sense of enjoyment in hobbies and activities? Are you having loss of appetite and weight, sleep difficulties and low energy, or self-destructive behaviour and suicidal ideation? Get help.

Anxeity Treatment

Do you often feel nervous, restless or tense? Are you having an increased heart rate, difficulty controlling worry, and trouble sleeping? Do you have trouble concentrating or thinking about anything other than the present worry? If yes, we can help.

Stress Management Therapy

Are you becoming easily agitated, frustrated, and moody? Are you having difficulty relaxing? Do you often feel bad about yourself, lonely, and a bit depressed? If yes, we can help you.

Pressure Management

Are you under any form of pressure to actualise a goal or feeling pressured over a failed task? It could be academics, financial, relationships, family related, from peers, and many more. If yes, make out time to see your counselor.

Bereavement & Grief Support

The feeling that comes with loss can be a very painful and overwhelming experience and can degenerate into health complications if appropriate care is not recieved. We know exactly how it feels that's why we are committed to help. Our search light may not have found you to bring us to you but trust us... We care. Connect with us let us help you.

Phobia Treatment

Do you have any irrational and uncontrollable fear(s)? It could be fear of crowd, talking in public, books, examination, height, strangers, cats, dogs, night, darkness, and many more. What ever phobia you have, our counselors can help you eliminate them.

PTSD Treatment

Ever heard about Post Traumatic Stress Disorder?

Are you constantly having intrusive thoughts of any painful events of the past, nightmares, and visual images of the same event? Are you losing memories and concentration ability? Do you often feel disoriented, confused, and unstable? Do you have mood swings? Let us help you.

Homesickness

It is a distress caused by being away from where you call home. It may leave you feeling withdrawn, isolated, anxious, and depressed. All of these effects can affect your behavioural pattern, health, and academics. Connect with us today. We can help.

Our Professional Training Services:

The Drive, Essence, And Target

With the increasing complexity of global culture due to cultural mixing owing to continued technological advancement in the area of communication and other rapid global changes, the human soceity has constantly remained plagued with continuous struggle for adaptation to meet the demands and pulls on its psychological well-being, where in, in the quest to adapt maximally, the need for mental health profession was birthed and thus the increasing demand for mental health practioners. Mental health care in its crude form has been a part of human existmence especially, talk therapy which has kept necessitating social ties and religion in part. However, as a profession it is relatively new in some parts of the world especially the second and third world nations and therefore lacks supposed popularity. Nevertheless, things are changing drastically and there's been an upsurge in the demand of its services but unfortunately, the profession is barely ready and still struggling to equip itself to be able to supply the needed manpower for its enormous task and thus, the wide divide between demand and supply of professionals to which quacks have exploited to defraud unsespecting victims with incompetent, fraudulent, and in most cases, services that could be harmful on the long run. It is for this reason that the Counseling Centre despite being a service centre, exteneded its scope to partnering with The Department of Guidance and Counselling of Delta State University, Abraka in training Guidance Counselors especially in the area of provision of opportuinity for counselling practicum thereby becoming as much a training centre in fashion to help surmount the bane of the profession and the world it is serving.

Are you passionate about helping people solve their problems? Are you already a trainee counselor? We can help you in your journey to becoming a professional counselor. We can help you gain professional competence to explore the yet untapped potentials and benefits of this young profession.

We accept undergraduate and post-graduate trainees up to doctorial level from other institutions of noble standing for intensive practicum exercise and also assist them in optaining license to practice and status.

Connect with us today through the office of the director let's help you gain professional competence.

Counseling Centre Handbook For Students And Staff

The counselling Centre Hanbook For Students And Staff is an eleven chapter handbook compiled and edited by Rev. Fr. Prof. Jude J. Obiunu, Acting Director, Student Counseling Centre with forward done by Prof. Andy O. Egwunyenga, Vice Chancellor, Delta State University. The book is meant to provide students and staff of the University with information about the counseling centre's activities on campus and also serve as a guide to fresh and stale students on how to adjust appropriately on campus.

The book throws light on various aspects of the Centre's services and provides a lot of useful information about how to student can access the universities services. Since its publication in 2021, it is provided to every student at the first point of contact with the university to ensure they receive appropriate guidance alongside other forms of orientation they will receive in time.
