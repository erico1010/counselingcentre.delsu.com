Relationship Problems

Relationships are beautiful and can come with its own unique challenges. We can help you through our relationship counseling services.

Academic Problems

Do you have any problems with your academics? Are you struggling with low academic grades? Are you having issues with poor comprehension? Do you you have exam phobia? We can help you through our academic counseling services.

Adjustment Problems

Cause: Poor transition between life stages, educational levels, residential enviroments, signifant events, relationships and many more. Untreated, they degenerate to further complications. If you have persistent symptoms of anxeity and depression after any transition make a date with us.

Depression

Are you having persistent low mood and feel irritable in most things? Are you begining to or already lost sense of enjoyment in hobbies and activities? Are you having loss of appetite and weight, sleep difficulties and low energy, or self-destructive behaviour and suicidal ideation? Get help.

Anxeity Problems

Do you often feel nervous, restless or tense? Are you having an increased heart rate, difficulty controlling worry, and trouble sleeping? Do you have trouble concentrating or thinking about anything other than the present worry? If yes, we can help.

STRESS

Are you becoming easily agitated, frustrated, and moody? Are you having difficulty relaxing? Do you often feel bad about yourself, lonely, and a bit depressed? If yes, we can help you.

PRESSURE

Are you under any form of pressure to actualise a goal or feeling pressured over a failed task? It could be academics, financial, relationships, family related, from peers, and many more. If yes, make out time to see your counselor.

GRIEF

The feeling that comes with loss can be a very painful and overwhelming experience and can degenerate into health complications if appropriate care is not recieved. We know exactly how it feels that's why we are committed to help. Connect with us let us help you.

PHOBIA

Do you have any irrational and uncontrollable fear(s)? It could be fear of crowd, talking in public, books, examination, height, strangers, cats, dogs, night, darkness, and many more. What ever phobia you have, our counselors can help you eliminate them.

TRAUMA

Are you constantly having intrusive thoughts of any painful events of the past, nightmares, and visual images of the same event? Are you losing memories and concentration ability? Do you often feel disoriented, confused, and unstable? Do you have mood swings? Let us help you.

HOMESICKNESS

It is a distress caused by being away from where you call home. It may leave you feeling withdrawn, isolated, anxious, and depressed. All of these effects can affect your behavioural pattern, health, and academics. Connect with us today. We can help.

SEXUALITY

Human sexuality is a broad field that hosts a wide range of problems. We have experienced counselors that will provide you with the assistance you need. Connect with us let us help you.

IDENTITY CRISIS

Do you often question your character and traits, have confusion about your purpose and passion, and experience anxeity? Do you have difficulty in maintaining your values in different situations? Do you have difficulty answering questions about yourself and trusting your ability to make decisions?

academic guidance

Our academic guidance services include a number of programmes aimed at making you reach optimal educational development by helping you discover, access, and develop the right skill set to exploit educational resources and opportunities around you. We do this by guiding you to have true self-knowledge along academic lines and translate same into making the most out of educational opportunities available in and out of campus.

Personal-Social Guidance

Personal-Social Guidance Services Our personal-social guidance services enables you to have self understanding with regards to relating with yourself and others in order to be able to deal effectively with and manage the psychological situations involved in interpersonal relationships. Life in and out of campus necessitates interaction and relationships hence the need for you to know yourself and relate accordingly. Lack of self-knowledge has led many into building relationships based on temporal passions or needs where in, they struggle to sustain when those passions die. It also manifests in building relationships along the lines of incompatibility. In other cases, many fail to make due inputs which they are capable of, put themselves under undue pressure trying to meet the expectation of others, over-expects from others, or on the worst case scenario, set unrealistic goals. Connect with us... let us help you have a full picture of yourself.

Vocational Guidance

Vocational Guidance Services Our vocational guidance services cover a wide range of programmes designed to help you assess your capabilities, interests, skills, aptitudes, and other qualities in order for you to know yourselves and make appropriate career choices. Many intending professionals are quick to choosing careers out of the blue only to find down the road that their choices do not permit them to fulfil their dreams. Some struggle to fit in, some under-perform, some quit, others never find gainful employment because the jobs they seek require a differnt kind persons that they are not despite the technical know hows they've acquired in the course of training or in many cases, their choice of profession are irrelevant in the society or the jobs they seek are non-existent in the economy. These have formed the basis for wide spread unemployment and under-employment across the globe. Hence, the need for vocational guidance.
